Can One Person Build a Billion-Dollar Startup?

EN

Andrew from General Intelligence Company of New York laid out a bold thesis: AI agents will soon enable a single person to run a billion-dollar operation. The vision — a one-person company at the top, managing a layer of specialized AI agents (customer support, finance, marketing) coordinated by "manager agents." Their first product, Co-Founder, lets you run agents in a cloud computer with persistent memory and drafts emails that actually sound like you. Key insight: agent coordination and the human-AI interface layer is where the real leverage lives.

中文

来自纽约 General Intelligence 公司的 Andrew 提出了一个大胆命题：AI agent 已基本可用，一个人运营十亿美元公司的时代即将到来。架构是这样的：顶层是一个人，下面是一层垂直 AI agent（客服、财务、营销），中间由"管理 agent"层协调调度。他们的首款产品 Co-Founder 在云端运行 agent、具备持久化记忆，甚至能模仿你的写作风格起草邮件。核心押注：做管理层和人机交互界面，而不是做每一个底层 agent。
